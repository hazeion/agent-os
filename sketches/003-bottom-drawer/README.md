## Variant: Bottom drawer

### Design stance
Use a secondary detail surface without redesigning the main column layout or interrupting flow with a modal.

### Key choices
- Layout: compact queue plus sticky bottom detail tray
- Typography: stable queue with a moderate reading surface below
- Interaction: click card, inspect detail in place, click another card to swap

### Trade-offs
- Strong at: quick comparisons, lower interruption, easier than a full split-pane redesign
- Weak at: persistent use of screen space and less room for future controls than a true inspector

### Best for
- A hybrid Mentat approach if we want more depth than a modal but less commitment than a dedicated right-side detail column
